June 2018 ~ October 2019

Mining Classification Rules for HIV-1 Protease Cleavage Sites Using Simplified Swarm Optimization

Generated a C-based computer program, utilizing simplified swarm optimization, to optimize an HIV-1 protease data set, containing protein octamer sequences, and to classify protease cleavage, achieving a prediction accuracy above 97%. Reliable predictors can contribute to increasing the specificity of protease inhibitors and improving antiretroviral therapy.
